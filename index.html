    Slitscanner is a little piece of Javascript you can run as a bookmarklet to start, well, slitscanning videos online. This only works in the HTML5 video players so for Vimeo you will need to select "Switch to HTML5 Player" in the lower right on the video pages. For YouTube it's a little trickier, as they force the Flash Player for videos with ads, but you can opt into the HTML5 test here.

    You can add it as a bookmarklet by dragging this link up to your bookmarks bar: slitscanner. Just start playing a HTML5 video on YouTube or Vimeo and then hit the bookmarklet, and it will start drawing onto a canvas in the browser. You can also download the code and modify it as you wish.
